PLACE YOUR PHOTO HERE
=====================
Save your profile photo as "photo.jpg" in this folder (assets/).

Then open index.html, find this comment:
  <!-- TO ADD PHOTO: delete the two lines above and uncomment: -->
  <!-- <img src="photo.jpg" ... -->

And update the src to: "assets/photo.jpg"

Recommended photo specs:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: at least 800x1000px
- Ratio: portrait (4:5)
- Focus: face centered and visible
